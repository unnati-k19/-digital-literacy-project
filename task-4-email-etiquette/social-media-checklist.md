SOCIAL MEDIA RESPONSIBILTY CHECKLIST


PRIVACY

Do's : Do review your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your posts.

Don'ts : Don't post sensitive personal information like your home address or phone number.


PROFESSIONALISM

Do's : Do curate a "clean" profile that reflects your interests and skills to future employers.	

Don'ts : Don't post content involving illegal activities, offensive language, or unprofessional behavior.


ENGAGEMENT

Do's : Do think for five seconds before hitting "post" or "comment" during a heated moment.	

Don'ts : Don't engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or spreading unverified rumors about others.


ACADEMIC INTEGRITY  

Do's : Do use social media to join study groups or follow educational tech influencers.

Don'ts : Don't post photos of exam papers, quiz answers, or confidential lab results.


SECURITY

Do's : Do use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

Don'ts : Don't click on suspicious links in DMs or tag your real-time location in every post.
